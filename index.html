Managers spend hours every week hunting down availability through group chats, personal texts, and word-of-mouth. Things get missed. Schedules get built on incomplete information.

A staff member texts a coworker, who texts the manager, who forgets to update the schedule. Shift goes uncovered. Manager finds out at the last minute. Chaos follows.

How ChoreoStaff Works

From empty calendar to fully staffed week, in three simple steps. No training required, no learning curve.

1

Manager Creates the Schedule

Build your shift schedule in advance. Set roles, hours, and team assignments. ChoreoStaff instantly flags any conflicts or coverage gaps before you publish.

2

Staff Submit Availability & Swaps

Your team submits their availability and requests shift swaps directly in the app, no texts, no group chats. Everything routes to the right place automatically.

3

Manager Approves, Conflicts Flagged

Review requests with full context and one-tap approvals. Any conflicts are highlighted automatically, so nothing slips through and nothing needs to be chased.

ChoreoStaff is a shift scheduling and operations platform built for managers and operators of shift-based businesses.
